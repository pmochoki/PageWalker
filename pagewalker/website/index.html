Pagewalker is a book discovery and social reading app: search and save titles, track what you read, leave reviews, and hang out in book clubs — without hosting copyrighted book text.

The Play badge links to your package id (com.pagewalker.app). The listing must be live in Play Console for the link to open the store page.

What you can do

Discover & collect

Find books via public catalogs, build your TBR, and organize reading, finished, and DNF lists.

Track reading

Sessions, progress, and wrapped-style stats so your year in books stays visible.

Social & clubs

Reviews, profiles, and book club rooms to discuss what you’re reading with friends.
